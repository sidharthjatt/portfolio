About

The person behind the work.

I'm Sidharth Choudhary, a final-year dual-degree student in Data and Computational Science at IIT Jodhpur, specializing in mathematics. I build machine learning and AI systems that go the full distance — from the math on paper to code that runs in production.

What I care about

Most student work dies in a notebook. Mine doesn't. I care about the whole path — clean data, models that earn their complexity, and systems that hold up when someone other than me uses them.

My focus right now is the newer wave of agentic AI: systems that don't just predict, but reason about a decision and can account for it. I'd rather understand why a model works than just call .fit().

Where I'm headed

I'm building toward roles in data science, ML engineering, and AI engineering — the kind of work where the math depth and the production thinking both matter. My background in quantitative modeling taught me to respect real data; agentic AI is where I want to push next.
